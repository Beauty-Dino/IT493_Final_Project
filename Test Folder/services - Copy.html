    Frequently Asked Questions

    A NON-REFUNDABLE DEPOSIT will be required for certain services when booking an appointment online to secure the spot. 100% of the deposit will go towards the total amount. The remaining balance will be paid on the appointment day. Any appointment without deposit in 48 hours would be CANCELLED

    Cancelling your appointment for any reason will result in losing your deposit.

    3 days notice is required if you need to reschedule an appointment to another date and time. Only TWO time rescheduling is allowed. If you fail to do so your deposit will be forfeited.

    Permanent makeup is NOT RECOMMENDED for anyone who is or has:
    - Pregnant or nursing
    - History of Keloids or Hypertrophic scarring
    - Diabetic (require doctor’s clearance)
    - Had Botox in recent 2 weeks

    BEFORE YOUR TREATMENT:
    - DO NOT take Aspirin, Fish Oil, Niacin, Vitamin E and/or Ibuprofen unless medically necessary 72 hrs prior to your procedure.
    - Discontinue Glycolics, Retin-A or Retinol products 2 weeks before the scheduled appointment.
    - DO NOT take any coffee in 24 hours before procedure.
